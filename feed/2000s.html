The Digital Revolution

The 2000s witnessed an unprecedented digital transformation that changed how we communicate, work, and entertain ourselves. From the rise of social media to the emergence of smartphones, this decade laid the foundation for our modern digital lifestyle.

Did you know? Facebook was founded in 2004, YouTube in 2005, and Twitter in 2006!

Key Technological Milestones

  • Rise of social networking platforms (Facebook, MySpace)
  • Introduction of the iPhone (2007)
  • YouTube and video sharing revolution
  • Transition from dial-up to broadband internet
  • Wikipedia and collaborative knowledge
  • Birth of the blogosphere
  • The iPod and digital music revolution
  • Early cloud computing
